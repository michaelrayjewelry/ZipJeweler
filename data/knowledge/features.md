# ZipJeweler — Features & Capabilities

## Inventory Management
- Track every piece of jewelry: rings, necklaces, bracelets, earrings, watches, loose stones
- Multi-location inventory (store, safe, showcase, consignment, repair bench)
- Barcode/SKU scanning and generation
- Automatic low-stock alerts
- Metal weight and stone tracking per piece
- Consignment tracking with auto-reconciliation
- Sync inventory across Etsy, Shopify, and in-store POS

## Order Management
- Custom order workflow: design → approval → production → delivery
- Repair ticket tracking with status updates
- Automated customer notifications at each stage
- Deposit and payment tracking
- Layaway management
- Rush order flagging and priority queuing

## Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
- Customer profiles with purchase history
- Anniversary and birthday reminders for upsell opportunities
- Automated follow-up after purchase or repair
- Customer wish lists
- Communication log (calls, emails, notes)
- VIP customer tagging and segmentation

## Production & Workflow
- CAD file handoff tracking (designer → production)
- Casting tree management and batch tracking
- Quality control checkpoints
- Production timeline with milestone tracking
- Vendor/supplier management for findings, stones, metals
- Work-in-progress (WIP) tracking per bench jeweler

## Pricing & Financials
- Metal price auto-updates (gold, silver, platinum, palladium)
- Stone pricing with markup calculator
- Labor cost tracking per piece
- Margin analysis per product line
- Quote generation for custom work
- Tax and duty calculations

## Repair Tracking
- Repair intake with photo documentation
- Status tracking: received → diagnosed → in progress → quality check → ready → picked up
- Parts and materials tracking per repair
- Warranty management
- Before/after photo comparison
- Automated "your repair is ready" notifications

## Multi-Channel Selling
- Etsy integration (inventory sync, order import)
- Shopify integration
- In-store POS compatibility
- Unified inventory across all channels
- Prevent overselling with real-time sync

## Reporting & Analytics
- Sales reports by period, product, category
- Inventory valuation reports
- Customer lifetime value analysis
- Repair turnaround time tracking
- Production efficiency metrics
- Profit margin analysis by product line
