# ZipJeweler — Customer Pain Points We Solve

## Pain Point: Inventory Chaos
**Who feels it**: Every jeweler with more than a handful of pieces
**The problem**: Tracking hundreds or thousands of pieces across showcases, safes, consignment locations, and online stores using spreadsheets, notebooks, or memory
**How ZipJeweler solves it**: Centralized inventory with barcode scanning, multi-location tracking, and automatic sync across Etsy/Shopify/POS

## Pain Point: Lost or Forgotten Custom Orders
**Who feels it**: Custom jewelers handling multiple commissions simultaneously
**The problem**: Losing track of which customer ordered what, what stage each order is at, whether deposits were collected, and when delivery is due
**How ZipJeweler solves it**: Custom order workflow with milestone tracking, automated customer notifications, deposit tracking, and deadline alerts

## Pain Point: Repair Ticket Nightmare
**Who feels it**: Jewelry repair shops and stores that do repairs
**The problem**: Paper tickets get lost, customers call asking for status updates, no way to track parts or labor per repair, no before/after documentation
**How ZipJeweler solves it**: Digital repair tracking with photo intake, status progression, automated "ready for pickup" notifications, and warranty management

## Pain Point: Pricing Headaches
**Who feels it**: Anyone who needs to price jewelry with fluctuating metal and stone costs
**The problem**: Manually looking up gold/silver prices, calculating material costs, adding labor and markup, creating quotes — and redoing it all when metal prices change
**How ZipJeweler solves it**: Auto-updating metal prices, stone pricing database, labor cost templates, instant quote generation with configurable markups

## Pain Point: Customer Follow-Up Falls Through the Cracks
**Who feels it**: Store owners who want repeat business
**The problem**: Forgetting to follow up after a sale, missing anniversary/birthday upsell opportunities, not knowing a customer's purchase history when they walk in
**How ZipJeweler solves it**: CRM with automated follow-ups, anniversary reminders, purchase history at a glance, and VIP customer segmentation

## Pain Point: Multi-Channel Selling Chaos
**Who feels it**: Jewelers selling on Etsy + Shopify + in-store
**The problem**: Selling the same piece on Etsy that was just sold in-store, manually updating inventory across platforms, orders scattered across systems
**How ZipJeweler solves it**: Real-time inventory sync across all channels, unified order management, automatic stock updates to prevent overselling

## Pain Point: CAD-to-Production Handoff
**Who feels it**: Jewelry designers working with separate production teams or casting houses
**The problem**: Emailing CAD files back and forth, losing track of which version was approved, no visibility into production progress after handoff
**How ZipJeweler solves it**: CAD file management with version tracking, approval workflows, production milestone tracking, and casting tree management

## Pain Point: Consignment Tracking
**Who feels it**: Jewelers with pieces on consignment at multiple locations
**The problem**: Not knowing which pieces are where, when consignment periods expire, reconciling what sold vs. what was returned
**How ZipJeweler solves it**: Consignment tracking with location, period, and auto-reconciliation when pieces sell or return

## Pain Point: Finding Reliable Manufacturers
**Who feels it**: Designers who need casting, finishing, or production services
**The problem**: No centralized way to find, vet, and manage relationships with casting houses and manufacturers
**How ZipJeweler solves it**: Vendor directory with rating system, order tracking per vendor, and quality history

## Pain Point: No Time for Marketing
**Who feels it**: Solo jewelers wearing all the hats
**The problem**: Too busy making jewelry, managing orders, and running the business to do marketing
**How ZipJeweler solves it**: (This is what the social media agents address — putting ZipJeweler in front of jewelers who are too busy to look for solutions)
