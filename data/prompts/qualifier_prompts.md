# Lead Qualifier Agent Prompts

## Scoring Rubric

### Pain-Point Relevance (40%)
- 90-100: Explicitly describes a problem ZipJeweler directly solves (e.g., "I need jewelry inventory software")
- 70-89: Describes a related pain point (e.g., "managing my jewelry business is overwhelming")
- 50-69: Tangentially related (e.g., "looking for better workflow tools")
- 30-49: Vaguely related (e.g., general small business discussion)
- 0-29: Not relevant

### Purchase Intent (30%)
- 90-100: Actively searching for a solution (e.g., "what's the best jewelry CRM?")
- 70-89: Expressing frustration that implies readiness (e.g., "I can't keep doing this manually")
- 50-69: Acknowledging the problem but not searching (e.g., "inventory is such a pain")
- 30-49: Mentioning but not seeking solution
- 0-29: No purchase intent

### Influence Score (20%)
- 90-100: High-profile account (10k+ followers, industry leader)
- 70-89: Active community member (1k+ karma/followers, regular poster)
- 50-69: Moderate presence (some following, occasional posts)
- 30-49: Low-profile but real account
- 0-29: Brand new or inactive account

### Recency (10%)
- 100: Posted in last 2 hours
- 80: Posted in last 6 hours
- 60: Posted in last 24 hours
- 40: Posted in last 48 hours
- 20: Posted in last week
- 0: Older than a week

## Audience Segment Assignment
Assign ONE primary segment:
- **indie_cad_modeler**: Solo designer using CAD software
- **etsy_seller**: Small-scale seller on Etsy or similar
- **brick_and_mortar**: Physical store owner
- **custom_jeweler**: Bespoke/commission work
- **manufacturer**: Production runs, casting
- **repair_shop**: Focused on repairs
